    Browse or brief.

    Explore the catalog above, or send over a moodboard, shot list or call-sheet. We'll suggest surfaces matched to palette, scale and lighting.

04 — Studio

A quiet studio on Broadview, built around one obsession — the surface in the shot.

The Surface Room began with a simple observation on set: the surface makes or breaks the image. Flat, plasticky, too uniform — and even the best food styling falls apart. So we started making our own.

Every piece in the current roster is designed, painted, aged and camera-tested in our Broadview studio — because a backdrop that doesn't hold up under strobes has no business being in the frame. Today the studio is a go-to resource for food stylists, commercial photographers and creative directors working on cookbooks, editorial features, product campaigns and restaurant launches across Canada.
